Step 1 — Identify Your Business Type

The first thing you need to do is select your business type in the Xendit dashboard. Selecting the wrong type is the most common reason applications are returned before review even begins.

Select your business type to see what applies to you:

PT (Perseroan Terbatas) & PT PMA

A legally registered company in Indonesia. Includes all company types with official government registration — PT, PT PMA (foreign-owned), and other registered entities.

Foundation / Association (Yayasan)

A non-profit organization registered for social, religious, educational, or humanitarian purposes.

CV (Commanditaire Vennootschap)

A partnership business with at least one active and one passive partner, registered with the government.

Perseroan Perseorangan (PP)

A sole proprietorship company (micro/small enterprise) registered under the 2020 Cipta Kerja law. Similar to a simplified limited liability company for individuals.

❓
My business type is not listed — what do I do?
Xendit accepts Business Entity (PT, CV, PT PMA, PP) and Foundation (Yayasan/Koperasi). Individual/personal accounts are not accepted. If your type is not listed, you must first register your business officially before applying.

Who Should Submit the Verification?

The application can be completed by an assigned PIC (admin, secretary, back-office). However, the Authorized Representative/Director(s) must personally complete the final Face Verification (Liveness Check).

💡
Liveness Check — Face Verification
The Authorized Representative/Director(s) must complete this step in person. It cannot be done on someone else's behalf. Your browser will ask for camera permission — you must click Allow. Use a device with a working front-facing camera.

Step 2 — Gather Your Documents

Before you open the Xendit dashboard, prepare all documents listed below and submit them in one complete submission to avoid delays.

💡
Tip: Scan or photograph each document clearly. Name the files so you can find them quickly. Having everything ready before you start will save you a lot of time.
ℹ️
New company with no amendments yet?
If your company was recently established (less than 5 years) and has never changed anything in its founding documents, you only need the Akta Pendirian and SK Menkeh. No Akta Perubahan required.

⚠️
If your application is fully rejected (rare)
In rare cases, an account may be fully rejected rather than returned for corrections. You will need to contact help@xendit.co to close the account before re-applying. A new application is not guaranteed to be approved.
